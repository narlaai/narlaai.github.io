About me

I care about making engineering work more understandable, repeatable, and useful.

I am currently studying Mechanical Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My interests sit at the overlap of physical systems and digital tools: modeling, automation, data analysis, and rapid prototyping.

What matters most to me is not just building something that works, but building something that is clear, efficient, and easy to improve over time.

This homepage is a simple place to collect my projects, interests, and ongoing learning.
